BITS

The first ever task-based intra-school ICT competition organized by ACICTS


What is BITS?

BITS 2018, the Intra-School practice-based ICT competition is organized by the Ananda College ICT Society for the first time in Sri Lanka with the intention of providing platform to our school students and polish their IT knowledge in the different fields of ICT.

80+ students

80+ Young Anandians have participated in BITS and 40+ out of them were able to successfully complete tasks upto now

200+ Tasks

200+ tasks were completed until now from blog post to creating website to developing FOSS.

13 mentors

Mentors are developers, designers, innovators and generally contributors who love to share and be a part of our open source community.
